Apple Pie Recipe

Auntie's Famous Apple Pie Recipe

Every year my aunt makes the best apple pie for the holiday seasons. She uses only the freshest ingredients which makes for a deliciously sweet masterpiece every time. All of the cousins devour the entire pie before dinner even begins. You are in for a real treat folks!

Ingredients

  • Refridgerated Pie Crust - 1 Pack
  • Unsalted Butter - 1/2 Cup
  • Flour - 3 Tbsp
  • Water - 1/4 Cup
  • White Sugar - 1/2 Cup
  • Brown Sugar - 1/2 Cup
  • Granny Smith Apples - 8

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stire in flour to form a paste. Add water, white sugar, and brown sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer.
  2. Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice work crust. Gently poor the sugar and butter liquid over the crust. Pour slowly so that it does not run off.
  3. Bake 15 minutes in the preheated overn. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees F. Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft.
